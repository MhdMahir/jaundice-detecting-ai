The Condition

What is Neonatal Jaundice?

Understanding the condition is the first step toward better outcomes for newborns.

visibility

Common Symptoms

Yellow discoloration of the skin and whites of the eyes — typically appearing within the first few days after birth.

science

Why It Happens

Excess bilirubin builds up in the blood because a newborn's liver isn't yet fully developed enough to process it efficiently.

medical_services

Why Early Detection Matters

While most cases resolve on their own, severe jaundice can cause neurological damage if untreated. Early screening enables timely intervention.
